About Us

We are a non-profit organization passionate about preventing the circulation and use of fake and counterfeit medicines. The issue of illegal pharmacies, medicine faking and counterfeiting have become too critical with dangerous effects and even death, this makes it complex for one agency or even a group of agencies to fight alone. We aim to do this by researching, educating, improving vigilance, empowering patients, and educating the public through new technologies. Let’s stop these fraudulent medications from harming anyone else.

TAKE THE DRUG TEST

Ensure that you are getting genuine medicine.


Send an SMS to 4544 on all the mobile numbers across Africa to find out if the medicine you have bought is genuine.


HOW IT WORKS

  1. Look for the scratch pad on the pack of the medicine.
  2. Scratch the pad to reveal the Serial ID.
  3. SMD to Serial ID to 1393(SMS FREE).
  4. Wait for a NO or OK response.
  5. If the response says NO, kindly send an SMS to the number 3434 or fill our complaint form below.
